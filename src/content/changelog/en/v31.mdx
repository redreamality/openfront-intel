## Major New Mechanics

- **Clan system**: A full <strong>clan management interface</strong> — browse, join, kick, ban, transfer leadership; open / invite-only modes; personal ranking lists sorted by game mode. Clan stats are tracked by clan tag (v31 doesn't enforce tag usage, v32 will).
- **Water Nukes**: A new game option where <strong>nukes turn land tiles into water</strong>. Maps may be completely reshaped by late game.
- **Lake / inland water trading**: Ports, trade ships, and Warships are <strong>no longer restricted to oceans</strong> — any body of water works. Caucasus, Great Lakes, and similar maps benefit directly.
- **Multi-Warship selection**: Shift+drag (or long-press) to <strong>batch-select Warships</strong> and command them together.
- **Warship retreat**: Damaged Warships <strong>auto-retreat</strong> (marked with a black cross in the center); target priority changed to Transport > Warship > Trade.
- **Warship port healing**: Friendly ports auto-heal nearby Warships, with healing slots scaling by <strong>port level</strong>.
- **Host cheats (private lobby)**: Toggle infinite gold, infinite troops, and starter resource packs.
- **Paid currency cosmetics**: In addition to Stripe, you can now spend <strong>in-game Plutonium / Caps</strong> on cosmetics.
- **Frontline troop hover**: Front-line tiles display <strong>real-time attacker / defender counts</strong>, color-coded to show who's winning.
- **Alliance / betrayal hotkeys**: K to request an alliance, L to break it — saves a menu expansion.
- **Sound effects (15)**: Full SFX set for nuke explosions, building, alliances, messages, and menus.

## Key Balance Changes

| Item | Old | New | Impact |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Kill payout (gold) | 100% | <strong>50%</strong> | Slows snowballing, large players can't sweep in one fight |
| SAM cooldown | 12 sec | <strong>9 sec</strong> | v30's 12 sec was too slow, dialed back to 9 |
| Silo cooldown | 7.5 sec | <strong>9 sec</strong> | Nuke pacing slowed overall |
| Nuke speed | 6 tile/tick | <strong>8 tile/tick</strong> | Reaction window shortened again |
| Port build time | 2 sec | <strong>5 sec</strong> | Naval openings are slower |
| Attacker loss ratio | Lower | <strong>60 / 40</strong> | Attackers pay slightly more than in v30 |
| Tribes | Standard strength | <strong>Weakened</strong> | Speeds up early expansion |
| Transport ship → auto-reject alliance | None | <strong>Sending transports auto-rejects incoming alliance requests</strong> | Fake alliance exploit continues to be patched |

## Nation AI

- Public-lobby Nations are no longer farmed for cities: when attacked, they build DefensePost instead of City.
- Hard / Impossible Nations on teams: automatically stop trading with the enemy, don't skip the strong, optimize save-up.
- In 5M Gold HvN, no more flash-build at the start.
- Port distribution is more even.

## Strategies Players Should Adjust

1. **Kill payout halved**: Eating a big player's gold only yields 50% — <strong>don't grind into a fortified player just for gold</strong>, let teammates / Nations bleed them for you.
2. **Water Nukes matches reshape terrain**: When this mode is on, maps may be completely redrawn in the late game. Plan a <strong>high-elevation safehouse</strong> in advance.
3. **Lakes are naval theaters too**: On maps like Caucasus / Great Lakes / Nile Delta, <strong>inland water combat</strong> becomes a routine tactic.
4. **Warships heal at ports**: Beyond trade income, ports' <strong>healing slots</strong> now matter for choosing when to retreat a Warship. Upgrade more ports, gain more safe harbors.
5. **K / L hotkeys**: High-level matches no longer need menu expansion to ally / unally — <strong>alliance tempo</strong> is faster.
