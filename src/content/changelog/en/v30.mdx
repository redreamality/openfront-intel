## Major New Mechanics

- **Three public queues**: The home page splits into **FFA / Teams / Special**. Special always carries 1–4 modifiers, cranking chaos to the max.
- **Visible lobby players and settings**: Just like private lobbies, <strong>public lobbies now show the current player list and room settings after joining</strong>.
- **Reconnect**: Network hiccups or accidental page refreshes no longer drop you from the match — <strong>rejoining the same game is supported</strong>.
- **Rail snapping**: Buildings placed on existing rails used to disconnect them — now they <strong>automatically split and connect</strong> into the existing network.
- **Attack panel rework**: The attack ratio bar is integrated into the unit display — centered, compact, mobile-friendly.
- **Player info panel moved to the top**: Mobile optimization.
- **Configurable Nation count (0–400)**: Single-player / private lobbies move from "Nations on / off" to a <strong>0–400 slider</strong>.

## Key Balance Changes

| Item | Old | New | Impact |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Trade ship global cap | ~100 | <strong>~400</strong> | v29's pathfinding optimization makes large-scale trade performance-viable |
| Train income curve | Linear | <strong>Diminishing from the 5th city station</strong> | Long rail chains stop generating runaway gold |
| Attacker losses | Defense-favored | <strong>Rebalanced (attack/defense ratio adjusted)</strong> | Committed attackers no longer hemorrhage as much |
| SAM cooldown | 7.5 sec | <strong>12 sec</strong> | Paired with v25/v27 SAM buffs, overall tactical pace slowed |
| 1v1 ranked map pool | Fully random | <strong>40% Australia / 20% Iceland / 20% Asia / 20% Europe Classic</strong>, 50% normal + 50% Compact | Tournament standard format |
| World map frequency | Normal | <strong>Sharply increased</strong> | Classic maps return to mainstream |

## New Modifiers (Public Special queue)

- **Crowded**: 125 players crammed into a small map, recreating the YouTuber brawl.
- **25M Starting Gold**: Everyone starts with 25M gold and a 2.5-minute spawn immunity prep window.
- **Hard Nations**: About 20% of HvN sets Nation difficulty to Hard.
- **Alliances Disabled**: All alliances forbidden.
- **x2 Gold**: Gold income doubled, fast-forward pace.
- **PVP Immunity**: In 5M Gold mode, adds 30 seconds of PVP immunity to prevent instant kills.

## Maps (12 new)

**Alps** (pure land, train trading), **The Box** (perfectly flat, pure land), **Hawaii**, **Traders Dream**, **Yenisei** (originally removed, scaled 3× down and back), **San Francisco**, **Arctic** (polar projection, Cold War NPCs), **Nile Delta** (with Suez Canal), **Passage**, **Bosphorus Straits**, **Bering Strait**, **Aegean** (Greek-Turkish, city-state NPCs).

## Nation AI

- Same-team Nations automatically stop trading with the enemy, no longer "skip the strong", and now <strong>save up reasonably</strong> for team combat.
- In 5M Gold HvN, Nations no longer flash-build at the start — the first building defaults to SAM.
- Port placement is more evenly distributed.

## Strategies Players Should Adjust

1. **Trade ship cap of 400 changes port strategy**: The v24-era "ports have sharply diminishing returns" logic flips — <strong>multiple ports + upgrades</strong> recombine into the optimal solution.
2. **Don't make rail chains too long**: Diminishing returns kick in at the 5th city — 3–5 station short chains + multiple parallel lines beat one long chain.
3. **SAM cooldown stretched to 12 sec**: Single SAM coverage density drops, so either <strong>stack multiples</strong> (range stacks since v27) or <strong>place multiples</strong> to form a relay.
4. **Special queue is for short casual matches**: FFA / Teams remain the formal modes, while stacked Special modifiers are for mindless brawls.
