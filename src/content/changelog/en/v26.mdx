## Major New Mechanics

- **New Build Bar**: Replaces the old radial submenu — buttons pinned to the bottom of the screen, common buildings one click away.
- **New player info panel**: The top-right player info card has been redesigned. Stats, diplomacy, and current troops are visible at a glance.
- **Resource gifting modal**: Right-clicking an ally opens a standalone window where you can send a specific amount of troops / gold in one go.
- **Rails cross rivers**: Train tracks can now span rivers, making long-distance rail networks far easier to build out.
- **AFK takeover**: Territory you couldn't get from active teammates can now be <strong>seized directly from disconnected teammates</strong>.
- **Delete units**: Regret a build? Right-click your own units to manually tear them down and recover part of the gold.

## Key Balance Changes

| Item | Old | New | Impact |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Teammate AFK | Only you keep playing | <strong>Team can take over their territory and ships</strong> | Reduces team losses from disconnects |
| MIRV warhead spread | Tighter | <strong>Wider outer ring scatter</strong> | More psychological pressure, harder to defend everything |
| Port trade ship rate | High | <strong>Lowered</strong>, paired with the v25 global cap | The gold ceiling from port count is pushed down further |
| Conspiracy traps | Ambush after betrayal → penalty | <strong>Attacking immediately after a new alliance counts as betrayal</strong> | Fixes the "fake alliance instant-snipe" exploit |

## Nation AI Improvements

- Nations make smarter overall decisions: caching player info, dynamically allying based on threat.
- No more "mindless nukes": targets are evaluated for value before launch.
- Nations on a team will actively support attacked teammates.

## Maps

- New **Japan** and **Montreal** maps (aotumuri / Duwibi).
- Map asset compression: client size from 140 MB → 30 MB (lossless).
- Fixed Strait of Gibraltar map boundary issue.

## Strategies Players Should Adjust

1. **Grab AFK teammates' territory**: From v26 on, idle teammates are effectively the team's reserve resource pool.
2. **Demolish-rebuild loop**: The unit deletion refund makes early-game experimentation cheaper — you can be more aggressive about placement.
3. **Rails string continents together**: With cross-river support, island and river-heavy maps can now also host factory networks.
